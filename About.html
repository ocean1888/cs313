About

Kyle DeWeese is a web-developer, and self proclaimed "Computer Nut" from Corona, California. Currently he attends BYU-I as a senior in Computer Science. While this site is intended to showcase his work, it will be lacking in some regards, as it doesn't contain many of his first works.

Kyle's first position in the realm of computers was as the lead CSS and Javascript programmer for an independant web development team. The small three man team was resposible for web creations over the course of about 6 months.

In his limited spare time, Kyle enjoys entertainment ranging from electronic to live productions. Kyle spent many years in theater productions, both on stage and behind the scenes. His other intrests include surfing, dancing, and he even works part time as a DJ.
